    About Us

    About JACHINDA FOUNDATION
    Founded in 2024, JACHINDA FOUNDATION is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) operating across Tanzania, with its head office in Iringa Region. We are a legally registered entity under the Non-Governmental Organizations Act, No. 24 of 2002, committed to transparency, accountability, and lasting community impact.

    Our Vision

    JACHINDA FOUNDATION exists to respond to social and economic matters, transform community members' lives in both rural and urban areas, and make people smile again.

    Our Mission

    We respect and value human dignity. We work for and with the poor and oppressed to promote human transformation, seek righteousness, and help communities achieve self-reliance while rebuilding hope.

    Who We Serve

    We focus on society's most vulnerable groups, including: Children, Youth, Women, Elderly, Orphans, Disabled persons, and marginalized communities. We embrace diversity our membership includes people of all genders, races, ethnicities, religions, and economic backgrounds.

    What We Do

    Community Empowerment

    We provide material support to reduce life hardship for orphans, elders, and disabled persons, ensuring their basic needs are met with dignity.

    Health Services

    We facilitate health-related support by providing medical equipment, health insurance, maintaining and rehabilitating health facilities, and assisting with ambulance services to ensure communities access quality healthcare.

    Agriculture & Environment

    We promote modern agriculture and breeding techniques through community education, while championing environmental conservation for sustainable livelihoods.

    Education Support

    We encourage education for children and youth by, establishing schools and vocational colleges, providing learning materials and scholarships, creating pathways for skills development.

    Sports & Advocacy

    We promote sports and games in rural and urban areas, using these platforms for charity outreach and supporting needy groups in our communities.

    Entrepreneurship Development

    We empower small producer groups especially in agriculture by providing knowledge, skills, and support to grow their enterprises and achieve economic independence and respond to climate and environment changes.

    Our Core Values

    • Human Dignity – Every person deserves respect
    • Transformation – Lasting change, not temporary relief
    • Self-Reliance – Empowering communities to stand on their own
    • Hope – Restoring optimism for a better future
    • Inclusivity – Serving all without discrimination

    Our Leadership

    JACHINDA FOUNDATION is governed by a dedicated Board of Directors and led by a team of office bearers. Our founding members bring passion, courage, and competence to every initiative we undertake.

    Our Reach

    While headquartered in Iringa Region (Mwenge street, nearby Lutheran church, KIHESA ward), our operations extend nationwide across Tanzania, serving both rural and urban communities where need is greatest.

    Accountability & Transparency

    • Regular financial reporting and independent audits
    • Annual General Meetings with all members
    • Clear conflict resolution procedures
    • Using all funds solely to promote our objectives
